    About Me

    Baking has always been something I love doing—there’s just something so satisfying about creating something from scratch and watching it come to life in the oven. Whether I’m making cookies, cakes, or experimenting with new recipes, I enjoy every step of the process. It’s not just about the end result for me, but about the joy and creativity that comes with it. Sharing my baked goods with friends and family is one of my favorite parts, as it’s a way to spread some happiness with a little sweetness.

    I take a lot of pride in perfecting my baking techniques, always looking for ways to improve. I know that using the right ingredients and getting the timing just right can make all the difference. It’s become more than just a hobby—it’s my way of expressing myself and relaxing. Every time I bake, I feel like I’m creating something special, and that’s a feeling I always look forward to.

    Travelling Adventures

    I have a deep love for traveling and exploring new places, which has taken me to 13 different countries so far. Each destination has offered its own unique experiences, from immersing myself in different cultures to trying new foods and meeting people from all walks of life. Traveling has broadened my perspective and fueled my curiosity about the world, making every trip a new adventure. It's one of my greatest joys, and I’m always looking forward to my next destination.

    Love for Music

    I absolutely love listening to music, and attending concerts is one of my favorite ways to experience it. The energy of live performances is something I can never get enough of, and I’ve had the incredible opportunity to see some of my favorite artists in person. One of my most memorable experiences was attending the Eras Tour not once, but twice, on two different continents. Traveling to different parts of the world to see such an iconic show was a dream come true, and each concert brought its own unique magic.

    My Athletic Journey

    I played field hockey for several years, and it became one of the most rewarding parts of my high school experience. My team was incredibly successful, going to states every year and winning districts consistently. The camaraderie, hard work, and dedication that came with being part of such a driven team left me with unforgettable memories. I learned so much about teamwork and perseverance, and those lessons continue to influence how I approach challenges in my life today.
