    Single Serve Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie

    Prep Time: 30 min Cook Time: 12 min Total Time: 42 min

    Yield: 1 cookie

    Try this simple classic chocolate chip cookie recipe for one!

    What are single serve cookies?

    A single serve cookie is a cookie designed to be baked individually, typically in a smaller portion, perfect for when you want just one fresh cookie without making a whole batch. It’s the ideal solution for satisfying a quick cookie craving without the temptation of leftovers. These cookies can be made from scratch or bought as dough balls for easy baking at home. Single serve cookies come in a variety of flavors and are great for personal indulgence or sharing with friends.

    Why exactly is this the best chocolate chip cookie recipe to exist?

    This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe because it combines the perfect balance of crispy edges and a soft, chewy center. The dough is made with a mix of brown and white sugars, which creates a rich, caramelized flavor that enhances the chocolate chips. It also includes a secret ingredient—chilled dough—that allows the flavors to develop fully and prevents the cookies from spreading too thin while baking. Finally, the use of high-quality chocolate chips ensures that every bite is packed with irresistible chocolatey goodness.

    Single Serve Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie

    Ingredients:

    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
    • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, packed
    • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
    • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • Pinch of baking soda (about 1/8 teaspoon)
    • Pinch of salt
    • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a small bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth
    3. Stir in the egg yolk and vanilla extract until well combined.
    4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt, mixing until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
    5. Scoop dough into 2 portions and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchemnt paper, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
    6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the center is still soft.
    7. Let the cookies cool completely for a few minutes before enjoying!
